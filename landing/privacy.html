Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2025

1. What We Collect

AgeGate collects minimal data by design. We store:

  • Developer accounts: Email, app name, Stripe customer ID, plan tier, MAU usage.
  • Verification requests: SHA-256 hash of user ID (not the actual ID), declared age, detected state, device platform, result (allowed/blocked/pending), timestamp.
  • Consent records: SHA-256 hash of user ID, consent method, consent token, parent email (encrypted at rest), expiration date, verification status.
  • API keys: Hashed key value, name, usage counters, timestamps.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store government ID photos. ID uploads are processed and immediately discarded after verification.
  • We do not store credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled by Stripe.
  • We do not track user behavior, location history, or app usage patterns.
  • We do not sell data to third parties. Ever.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely for:

  • Age verification and parental consent compliance
  • Audit logging for legal compliance (state laws require retention)
  • Billing and plan limit enforcement
  • Service improvement and debugging

4. Data Retention

Retention periods are set per state law and are configurable in your dashboard:

  • Texas: 365 days (or until consent expires, then deleted)
  • California: 365 days
  • Louisiana: 180 days
  • Utah: 90 days

Consent records are hard-deleted after expiration. Verification requests are soft-deleted (marked but recoverable for 30 days for audit disputes, then purged).

5. Security

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) in PostgreSQL ensures developers only see their own data.
  • API keys are hashed with SHA-256 and never stored in plaintext after initial creation.
  • Infrastructure hosted on Supabase (AWS/GCP) with SOC 2 Type II compliance.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Export your data
  • Correct inaccurate data

Contact privacy@agegate.dev for data requests.
